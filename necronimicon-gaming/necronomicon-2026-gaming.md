## Registration

Player registration for all games can be made **pre-convention** via email to `gaming@necronomicon-providence.com` or **during the convention** at any Minion station on the **18th & 17th floors of the Providence Graduate: Biltmore**.

**All player registration requests are first-come-first-served.**
